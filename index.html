My Services

Comprehensive post-production solutions customized to your specific needs.

YouTube Video Editing

Optimized for retention. I provide snappy cuts, engaging B-roll overlays, pop-up text graphics, sound effects, and background music tailored to your niche.

Shorts & Reels

High-retention vertical content for TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts with engaging captions and fast pacing.

Travel Vlog Editing

Cinematic travel montages, dynamic speed ramping, map animations, and immersive drone footage coloring.

Commercials & Promo

Cinematic editing for brands and businesses. High-end visual storytelling to elevate your product or service and drive conversions.

Motion Graphics & Titles

I build custom templates, lower thirds, and intro sequences in After Effects to give your videos a premium, polished look.

Podcast Editing

Multi-cam switching, background noise removal, audio sweetening, and engaging highlight clips from long unscripted sessions.
